What AI Code Review Does Well

  • Learning: Integrates with your codebase conventions over time

Best Practices for AI-Assisted Reviews

  1. Use AI for the first pass. Let it catch formatting, simple bugs, and security issues automatically.
  2. Review AI suggestions critically. AI is a tool, not an authority.
  3. Train your team. Document which AI suggestions to accept automatically and which need discussion.
  4. Integrate into CI/CD. Block merges only on high-severity findings.
  5. Measure impact. Track review time before and after AI adoption.
