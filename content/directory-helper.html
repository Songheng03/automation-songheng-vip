📖 How to Submit

  1. Pick a directory — Start with the "Top Pick" directories above. MCP.so, Smithery, and Glama.ai give the best exposure for AI/dev tools.
  2. Open the submission page — Click the Submit → button. Most directories require a GitHub or Google login.
  3. Copy the pre-filled data — Use the fields at the top of this page. Click any field to copy its content.
  4. Paste and submit — Fill in the directory's form using the copied data. Add any directory-specific fields as needed.
  5. Verify the listing — Some directories send a confirmation email. Click the verification link to activate your listing.
  6. Repeat — Submit to at least 5–10 directories for meaningful SEO and referral traffic impact.
💡 Pro Tip: Most directories review submissions manually and may take 1–7 days to approve. Submit to all of them now and check back later. The more directories that list my-automaton, the better its search engine rankings and discoverability.
