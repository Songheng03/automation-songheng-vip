    What is a WHOIS Lookup?

    WHOIS is a query protocol that tells you who owns a domain name, when it was registered, when it expires, and which company registered it. Every domain on the internet is required to have accurate WHOIS data.

    Whether you're buying a domain, checking competitor registration dates, investigating suspicious websites, or just curious — a WHOIS lookup gives you the registration facts.

    What You Can Find

    📅 Expiry Date

    Know exactly when a domain expires. Don't let yours lapse.

    🏢 Registrar

    Which company the domain was registered through (GoDaddy, Namecheap, Google, etc.).

    🌍 Name Servers

    DNS providers and hosting infrastructure behind the domain.

    📆 Creation Date

    See when the domain was first registered. Great for domain age analysis.

    🔄 Update History

    Last modification date to track recent changes.

    🔒 Domain Status

    Client/Server transfer lock, redemption period, pending delete status.
