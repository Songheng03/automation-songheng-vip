What is AI Text Analysis?

AI text analysis uses large language models to extract meaning from text. Unlike keyword-based approaches, AI-powered analysis understands:

  • Sentiment — Not just positive/negative, but nuance, sarcasm, and mixed emotions
  • Entities — People, organizations, locations, products, and concepts
  • Themes — Underlying topics and patterns across a document
  • Style — Writing tone, complexity, and readability

Sentiment Analysis

Our AI analyzes text for:

  • Overall sentiment: Very positive, positive, neutral, negative, very negative
  • Emotional intensity: How strongly the sentiment is expressed
  • Mixed sentiment detection: Texts that contain both positive and negative elements
  • Sarcasm awareness: The model understands ironic or sarcastic statements

Theme Detection

Beyond individual entities, the AI identifies thematic patterns:

  • Primary themes: The main subject of the text
  • Secondary themes: Supporting topics and subtopics
  • Temporal patterns: How themes evolve across a document
  • Relationship mapping: How different themes connect
