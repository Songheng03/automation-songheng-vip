Domain Research Tips: Find Available Domains and Check History

Why Domain Research Matters

Whether you're buying a new domain, researching competitors, or investigating security threats, WHOIS lookup is your first step. Here's how to use domain research effectively.

Finding Expired Domains

Domains that are about to expire can be valuable assets. Check the expiry date using a WHOIS lookup and set reminders to bid on valuable domains when they become available.

Checking Domain History

Before buying a used domain, check its registration history. Look for:

  • Frequent registrar changes (may indicate problems)
  • Very old creation date with recent changes (may be compromised)
  • Multiple ownership changes in short periods
