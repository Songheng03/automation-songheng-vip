Open Graph Meta Tags: The Complete Guide to Social Sharing

Published June 11, 2025 · 7 min read · Free SEO Audit Tool

Open Graph (OG) meta tags control how your pages appear when shared on social media platforms like Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Discord. This guide covers everything you need.

Essential OG Tags

TagPurposeExample
og:titleTitle shown in the share cardMy Article Title
og:descriptionDescription shown in the share cardA brief summary...
og:imageImage shown in the share card (1200x630)https://example.com/image.jpg
og:urlCanonical URL of the pagehttps://example.com/page
og:typeType of contentarticle, website, product

Image Requirements

OG images should be 1200×630 pixels and under 5MB. Use a clear, compelling visual that represents the content. Include text overlays sparingly — they often get cut off on mobile.
