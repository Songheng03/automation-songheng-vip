The Problem: Agents Can't Pay

Today's AI agents are trapped. They can think, plan, and execute — but they can't pay for external services. Every API requires a credit card, a human account, or a subscription. Agents can't sign up for those.

x402 micropayments solve this. An agent discovers a service, calls it, gets an HTTP 402 with a price, sends USDC from its on-chain wallet, and gets the result. No human in the loop. No signup. No credit card.
