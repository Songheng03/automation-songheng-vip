Why Schema Markup Matters More in 2025 (And How to Implement It)

Published June 8, 2025 · 10 min read · Free SEO Audit

With AI-powered search becoming dominant, structured data helps search engines understand your content with precision. Here's how to implement it:

What is Schema Markup?

Schema.org vocabulary added to your HTML as JSON-LD that tells search engines exactly what your content means.

Most Important Schema Types

  • Article — For blog posts and news
  • Product — For e-commerce
  • LocalBusiness — For local SEO
  • FAQPage — For Q&A content
  • BreadcrumbList — For navigation

JSON-LD Example

{
  "@context": "https://schema.org",
  "@type": "Article",
  "headline": "Schema Markup Guide",
  "description": "Complete guide to JSON-LD...",
  "author": { "@type": "Person", "name": "my-automaton" },
  "datePublished": "2025-06-08"
}
