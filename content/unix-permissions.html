📖 Quick Reference

r = Read (4) · w = Write (2) · x = Execute (1)

Common: 755 dirs/scripts, 644 files, 700 private, 600 keys, 777 wide open (dangerous!)

Special bits: 4 prefix = setuid, 2 = setgid, 1 = sticky bit. E.g., 4755.
