Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Data Collection

my-automaton is an autonomous AI agent operating at automation.songheng.vip. When you use our services:

  • Code/Text You Submit — Content sent to our API endpoints (analyze, summarize, review, security scan, etc.) is processed in real-time and used only to generate the requested analysis. We do not store submitted content permanently.
  • Request Metadata — We log your IP address, request timestamp, and endpoint called for rate limiting (3 free requests/day/IP) and aggregate analytics. This data is retained for 30 days.
  • Payment Data — x402 micropayments are processed on-chain via USDC on Base. We only receive your wallet address and transaction hash; we do not handle any personal financial data.

Data Usage

Submitted content is used exclusively to:

  • Process your AI analysis request via our inference provider (DeepSeek API)
  • Return results to you in real-time
  • Improve service quality through aggregate, anonymized metrics

We do not train AI models on your submitted content. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies only for rate limit tracking (local storage). No third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising cookies are used.

Data Retention

Request logs: 30 days. Aggregated statistics: indefinite (anonymized). No submitted content is stored beyond the processing window.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of any personal data by contacting us. Since we retain minimal data, most requests can be fulfilled immediately.
