🚀 Submission Helper

Submit your website to search engines and web directories in one click — links are pre-filled with your domain.

📋 How to Use

  1. Enter your full domain above (e.g. mybusiness.com).
  2. Click "Update All Links" — every submission link updates instantly.
  3. Click each link below to open the search engine's submission page with your domain pre-filled.
  4. Follow the on-page instructions for each engine to complete the submission.
  5. Share this page with colleagues so they can submit too!
